"Who is Angela Means"

"From Hollywood screens to literary dreams and culinary scenes"

From Screen to Page to Kitchen

You may know me from my memorable roles in cult classic films like FRIDAY or from my work on the beloved children's show Cousin Skeeter. But my creative journey has taken me far beyond acting.

As an author, I've channeled my love for storytelling into both whimsical children's books that spark young imaginations and chilling horror novels that keep readers up at night. Each genre allows me to explore different facets of the human experience.

In the kitchen, I bring the same passion and creativity to my culinary creations. Food, like storytelling, is about connection, tradition, and innovation. Whether I'm developing a new recipe or writing my next book, I'm always seeking to create experiences that resonate deeply.
